Persona 2: Mark

Persona Type: Business Owner

  • Name: Mark
  • Age: 45
  • Location: Des Moines, Iowa
  • Technical Comfort: Intermediate
  • Profession: Owns a Bakery
"I need a website that’s easy to set up and shows my customers what we offer. I’m not a tech expert, so it has to be simple but professional enough to help my business grow."

Back Story

  • Lives with his wife and 2 kids.
  • Is the sole earner of his family.
  • Relies on word of mouth and sometimes fliers to promote his business.
  • Wants a straightforward website to help direct customers to his bakery.

Frustrations

  • Feels overwhelmed by the number of tools, platforms, and web hosts out there. Doesn't know which one to pick.
  • Has struggled in the past with software that needs to be maintained.

Motivations

  • Wants to set up a simple website to show his menu, location, store hours, and any promotions or special events.
  • Wants a fast and easy way to get the website up and running without having to learn new programming skills.
  • Wants a website that is accessible from all devices, to increase potential customer base.

Ideal Experience

  • Needs a platform with premade templates that is highly customizable to ensure a quick production.
  • Any questions or concepts that are interesting can be researched with the available resources like W3Schools and GeeksforGeeks.
