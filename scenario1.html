Scenario 1: Creating a Website Easily

Emma's Journey

This flowchart demonstrates Emma's process for creating her personal website with ease. It highlights the steps she takes to choose a platform, customize a template, and publish her site without needing extensive technical knowledge. The access to the technical knowledge and skills she needs to take it to the next level is at the tip of her fingers with plentiful resources to learn.
