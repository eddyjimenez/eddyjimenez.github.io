Persona 1: Emma

Persona Type: Artist

  • Name: Emma
  • Age: 32
  • Location: Portland, Oregon
  • Technical Comfort: Beginner
  • Profession: Freelance Illustrator
"I want a website that shows off my art, but I don't know where to start. I need something simple that helps me bring my creative vision to life without feeling overwhelmed by technical details."

Back Story

  • Lives alone with her cat.
  • Very little web development skills.
  • Creates digital art for children's books and small businesses.
  • Has always relied on social media to promote her art.

Behaviors

  • Gets easily confused with too much CS jargon.
  • Has limited time to learn due to her schedule.

Motivations

  • Wants to create a personal website to better promote her art.
  • Values clean and appealing front-end design.
  • Prefers step-by-step guidance to help build her website.

Ideal Experience

  • Needs a simple clean interface that includes drag-and-drop features, and countless tutorials.
  • Able to create the idea in her head without spending too much time.
